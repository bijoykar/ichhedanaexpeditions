## Key URL Patterns

### Structure Analysis:
- **Base Domain**: `ichhedanaexpeditions.com`
- **Tour URLs**: `/tour/[tour-name]` or `/tours/[tour-name]`
- **Destination URLs**: `/destination/[destination-name]`
- **Static Pages**: `/[page-name]/`
- **Policy Pages**: `/policy/[policy-type]`

### URL Naming Convention:
- Lowercase with hyphens
- Descriptive slugs
- Duration indicators in tour URLs (e.g., `---3-nights---4-days`)
- Location-based destination slugs
